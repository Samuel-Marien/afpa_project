## Goal

Alumni-type platform project (alumni networks) specializing in AFPA tech training.
The objective is to become a platform for exchange and making contacts between students, trainers and recruiters.
Through a series of communication and exchange tools, such as messaging, creation of a thematic group, presentation sheets, etc. Users will be able to build up a network of friends and relationships to enrich and facilitate their professional project.
